About This Blog

Living Word of Life was born out of a deep love for God’s Word and a desire to share its truth and encouragement with others daily.

This blog is maintained by a loving grandma who has spent many years meditating on the Scriptures, and her grandchild, who helps share her daily insights and devotionals online for the world to see.

Here, you’ll find daily devotionals, Bible verses, and spirit-filled reflections to uplift, strengthen, and draw you closer to Jesus.

We pray this site becomes a place of peace and blessing for all who visit. Thank you for stopping by!
